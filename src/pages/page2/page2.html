Weighing it up

Anybody who has used self-harm has done this for a reason... often because of not knowing what else to do to feel better. However, we know that even though it can help, there are also often some things about self-harm that can make people feel worse again.

Can you think of some good things, and not so good things about self harm? Try to weight it up!
