Hope not Harm

Shift your attention

Grounding exercise, shift your attention to:

5 things you can see

4 things you can hear

3 things you can touch

2 things you can smell

1 thing you can taste

 Remember It’s ok not to feel ok sometimes. We all have emotions that we find difficult and upsetting. These can take some practice... don’t worry if they don’t work first time! Give them a go again and see what helps.
