Hope not Harm

Some ideas of other ways of helping myself feel better

Phone/ talk to someone you feel close to—even a general conversation may help you to feel calmer. Arts and crafts—spending time making things may help you to feel calmer and give chance to move away from difficult thoughts. Listening to happy or comforting music. Write your thoughts or feelings down—keep them or rip up the paper and throw it away. Throwing them away can help you to remind yourself that you can take control of your thoughts. Going for a walk or try out some other exercise. Going to a place where you feel safe. Watch a film or TV show that you enjoy. Having a relaxing bath or shower.
